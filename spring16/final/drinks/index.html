Green & White Teas

Green tea is steamed or work fried after harvest to half oxidation. It is criso and refreshing with a delicate vegetal flavor, low in caffeine compared to other teas, and high in cancer preventing antioxidants. Green tea is often compared to a super food containing B-complex, Vitamin C, poly phenols and more. White tea is the least pro cessed type of tea. It is air-dried to preserve all its nutrients and antioxidants. Urth's green and white teas are air shipped fresh from Japan and Cjina all year long. To brew bring water to 170 degrees, or as a Chinese tea apostle called "Fish eyes" (for small bubbles on the bottom of the pot) and steep for 1 minute only.
